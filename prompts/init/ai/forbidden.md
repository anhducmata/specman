# Forbidden Patterns

> ⚠️ DRAFT — List patterns and practices that AI tools must avoid.

## Never Do
- Do not store secrets in code or specs
- Do not bypass authentication or authorization
- Do not ignore error handling
- Do not change domain rules without explicit approval
- Do not remove or weaken security controls
- Do not store PII or customer data in solved cases
- Do not store raw production logs in solved cases

## Avoid
- Avoid over-engineering simple solutions
- Avoid introducing new dependencies without justification
- Avoid breaking existing tests
